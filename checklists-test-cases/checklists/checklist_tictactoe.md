# Checklist for Tic-Tac-Toe game (playtictactoe.org)

- [ ] Pressing the speaker button turns the sound on and off
- [ ] Clicking on the logo icon at the top left opens the link in a new tab
- [ ] Clicking on a cell places an icon in it + the icon corresponds to the one indicated in the bottom panel
- [ ] Clicking on the bottom panel switches the mode (Player/Computer or two players)
- [ ] The computer moves after the player’s turn only once
- [ ] In two-player mode, the icons placed in the cells (X and O) alternate
- [ ] In case of a draw, 1 point is added to the middle scoreboard
- [ ] If a player wins, 1 point is added to his counter
- [ ] At the end of the game, the icons on the field turn gray, except for the row of three icons
- [ ] When you click on the field after the end of the game, the field is cleared and a new game starts
- [ ] At the beginning of the second and subsequent rounds the score is not reset
- [ ] When the page is refreshed, the score is reset
- [ ] Game stays in the middle when resizing browser window
- [ ] Game stays in the middle when scaling
- [ ] When you press Tab, the game cells are highlighted in order from left to right and top to bottom
- [ ] When you press Tab, the sound button is not highlighted (bug?)
- [ ] When you press Tab, occupied game cells are also highlighted (bug?)
